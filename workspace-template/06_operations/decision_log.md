# Decision Log

Purpose: Record important decisions, rationale, and ownership.

## Decision Entry Format
### YYYY-MM-DD - Decision title
- Owner:
- Decision:
- Why:
- Options considered:
- Follow-up:

## Example
### 2026-03-27 - Start with one ICP
- Owner: Producer
- Decision: Focus initial strategy on gift-oriented buyers.
- Why: It creates clearer positioning and faster validation.
- Options considered: Broad home decor audience, wedding segment, gift-first segment.
- Follow-up: Update positioning and content plan.
