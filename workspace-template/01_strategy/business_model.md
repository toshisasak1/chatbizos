# Business Model

Purpose: Define how the business creates value, delivers value, and captures revenue.

## Revenue Model
- Example: Direct-to-consumer product sales through a simple storefront.

## Cost Structure
- Example: Product materials, packaging, shipping, payment processing, content creation tools.

## Key Assumptions
1. Customers are willing to pay a premium for design and giftability.
2. Repeat purchase is possible through seasonal collections.
