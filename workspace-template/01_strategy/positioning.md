# Positioning

Purpose: Clarify how the business should be perceived relative to alternatives.

## Positioning Statement
- Example: A design-forward candle brand that makes premium gifting feel easy and personal.

## Alternatives Customers Compare Against
- Big marketplace sellers
- Mass-market home goods brands
- Local handmade shops

## Differentiators
1. Curated scent profiles
2. Gift-ready presentation
3. Clear product storytelling
