# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

Purpose: Define the most promising customer segment in enough detail to guide strategy and messaging.

## Primary ICP
- Example: Women aged 28-42 buying premium home and gift products online.

## Pain Points
1. Wants thoughtful gifts without decision fatigue.
2. Prefers premium aesthetics but does not want luxury-store friction.

## Buying Triggers
- Example: Birthdays, holidays, home refresh, self-care routines.
