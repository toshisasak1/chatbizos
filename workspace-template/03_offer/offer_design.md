# Offer Design

Purpose: Define what is being sold, for whom, and why it is compelling.

## Core Offer
- Example: Premium handmade candles designed for gifting and small home rituals.

## Offer Components
1. Signature candle line
2. Gift-ready packaging
3. Seasonal bundles

## Promise
- Example: A premium, easy-to-gift product that feels personal without requiring gift expertise.
