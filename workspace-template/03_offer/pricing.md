# Pricing

Purpose: Document pricing options, rationale, risks, and validation plan.

## Pricing Options
| Option | Description | Intended Buyer | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Conservative | Lower introductory pricing | First-time buyers | May compress margins |
| Balanced | Mid-range premium pricing | Core ICP | Best default |
| Aggressive | Higher premium positioning | Gift buyers seeking prestige | Higher conversion friction |

## Recommendation
- Example: Start with balanced pricing and test gift bundles for AOV growth.
